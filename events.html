Musical Chairs

Time
Day 2:- 10:00 - 11:00
Venue
Lawn Tennis Ground
Coordinators
Shivansh Tiwari
Anushka Agarwal
Description
Music and games are natural complements to one another the way suits and ties are to Justin Timberlake. The relationship between the two is so tight that it’s no surprise to see how music has become engrained in the tradition of many fun events. Musical Chair is the first one on your list to cross off, so don't miss the opportunity to be a part of the fun and music together.

Musical chairs is a game where a number of chairs, one fewer than the number of players, are arranged facing outward with the players standing in a circle just outside the chairs. Usually music is played while the players in the circle walk in unison around the chairs. When the music stops each player attempts to sit down in one of the chairs. The player who is left without a chair is eliminated from the game. One chair is then removed/eliminated to ensure that there will always be one fewer chair than there are players. The music resumes and the cycle repeats until there is only one player left in the game, who is the winner.

Survival War

Time
Day 2:- 5:00 - 5:30
Venue
Lawn Tennis Ground
Coordinators
Divyanshu Singh

Shivam Kumar
Description
The event worth waiting for Ruchihaar'18. One of the most awaited fun event is here,with a blend of fun and strength. "Winning is only half of it.Having fun is the other half".

Survival War, also known as shoulder wars, is an informal game, characterized by one team member sitting on the shoulders of his or her teammate or riding piggy-back. The object of the game is to knock down or separate an opposing team through team effort. The person on top is considered to be the "attacker" while the person below is considered to be the "vehicle". The person below may not use arms or hands and must rely on momentum to attack by running into the other team. The person on his/her shoulders is the "attacker" and may use any means possible of separating the other team or knocking them to the ground. If a team is separated or knocked down in any way, they are required to resign from the game and the last team to remain together is considered the winner.
